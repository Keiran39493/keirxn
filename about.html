About

keirxn

A private personal dashboard for tracking health, fitness, and nutrition. It combines live Garmin biometric data with age-adjusted health analysis and a curated recipe collection — all in one place.

Dashboard

The dashboard pulls live data from Garmin Connect and presents today's metrics at a glance, alongside 7-day trend charts for the most important recovery and performance signals.

HRV
Heart rate variability — the primary readiness signal
Resting HR
Beats per minute at complete rest
VO₂ Max
Aerobic capacity and fitness age estimate
Body Battery
Garmin energy reserve score (0–100)
Sleep Score
Overall sleep quality based on duration and stages
Stress
Daily average stress level from HRV patterns
Steps
Daily movement against your step goal
SpO₂
Blood oxygen saturation percentage
Calories
Active calories burned throughout the day
Respiration
Average waking and sleep breathing rate

The dashboard also generates automatic insights — flagging when HRV drops below your weekly baseline, when sleep is insufficient, when body battery is critically low, or when stress is elevated — each with a specific recommended action.

Insights

The Insights page puts your metrics in context. Enter your age, height, weight, and gender to unlock age-adjusted analysis across four key health markers.

  • HRV — compared against population percentiles for your age group, with a target to move from your current percentile to the next tier
  • Resting Heart Rate — lower is better; scored against age norms with elite athlete benchmarks for context
  • Body Battery — colour-coded energy level with guidance on how to read and maximise your daily score
  • Sleep Score — percentile ranking with evidence-based tips on sleep hygiene and recovery

Each metric card includes a plain-English explanation of what the number means, a personalised next goal, and a practical list of ways to improve it. BMI is calculated automatically from your height and weight in either metric or imperial units.

Recipes

A private, searchable collection of curated recipes — from quick weekday meals to weekend centrepieces. Each recipe shows a photo, dietary tags, prep and cook times, serving count, calories, and full ingredients and method.

  • Full-text search across recipe names and ingredients
  • Dietary filters: vegan, vegetarian, high-protein, low-carb, and more
  • Responsive card grid — works on phone, tablet, and desktop
  • Detailed recipe view with step-by-step instructions

How it works

keirxn is a lightweight Flask application served via Gunicorn, deployed on Render. The frontend is plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks. Data flows from Garmin Connect via the garminconnect Python library and is cached for 30 minutes to avoid hitting rate limits.

  • Authentication is handled client-side with a SHA-256 hashed passphrase and 8-hour session expiry
  • Garmin OAuth tokens are stored locally and used for silent reconnection on startup
  • Profile data (name, age, height, weight) is persisted in a local JSON file
  • Dark mode is stored in localStorage with an anti-flash inline script to prevent a white flicker on load
  • Hosted at keirxn.com via Namecheap DNS pointing to Render
